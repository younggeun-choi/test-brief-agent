- 발행: 2026-06-03
- 본문:
  Google announced compliance with the U.K.'s regulatory requirements, which state that the tech giant must offer publishers a way to opt out of being aggregated into AI search.
  Publishers will be able to use a new toggle in Google's Search Console to opt out, after which the publisher's site will not be shown in Google's generative AI Search features, like AI Overviews, AI Mode, or AI Overviews in Discover.
